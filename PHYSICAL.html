Physical Conditioning in Blue Lock

Conditioning Aspect Description Benefit Picture
Strength Training Focuses on building muscle strength and endurance. Improves physical resilience and power during matches.
Agility Drills Enhances quick direction changes and body control. Boosts responsiveness and reduces injury risk.
Cardiovascular Training Improves overall stamina and endurance. Maintains performance throughout the game.
Flexibility Exercises Increases joint mobility and muscle elasticity. Prevents injuries and improves range of motion.
